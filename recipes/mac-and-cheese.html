Description

classic mac & cheese recepie

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pasta
  • 1 ounce Cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1 tablespoon Cheddar cheese powder
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons everything seasoning blend

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook until tender with a bite, about 7 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile in a food processor add cottage cheese, cheddar cheese, parmigiano reggiano, chedar cheese powderm worcestershire, dijon and milk. Season with salt and peppar. Blend until smooth.
  3. Drain pasta and return pasta to pot. Pour in cheese sauce. Add everything bagel seasoning and stir to combine.
