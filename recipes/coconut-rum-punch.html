Coconut rum punch

home

Description

Tasty rum drink with cocnut flavor

Ingredients

  • ice
  • 2 fuild ounces rom
  • 2 fluid ounces pineapple coconut juice
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon agave syrup
  • ginger ale or lemon lime soda
  • 1 lime slice
  • 1/3 pineapple ring

Directions

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice, add rum, cococut pineapple juice. lime juice and agave syrup. Shake until the outside of the shaker is forsted, about 30s
  2. Pour into a glass filled with ice, and top off with ginger ale. Garnish with lime and pneapple
